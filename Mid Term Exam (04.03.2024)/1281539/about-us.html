About Us

The programme aims to enhance the employment prospects of Muslim youths (who come from financially disadvantaged backgrounds) from different non-IT education backgrounds like Science, Humanities, Business and Madrasah. The programme imparts professional level IT training of global standard to ensure gainful employment of the scholarship winners.
The IsDB-BISEW IT Scholarship Programme commenced in 2003. The programme has established itself as a reliable source of IT professionals for recruiters in the country.
